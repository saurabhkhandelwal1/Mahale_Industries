Mahavir Double filtered groundnut oil

Mahavir Double Filtered Groundnut oil is filtered two times through hard cloth or others equipment to remove the solid particles and contaminants from the oil but no chemicals are used in the process. They are generally dark and cloudy in appearance and have a pure and particular smell of Groundnut Seeds.

Mahavir's Kachi Ghani Groundnut Oil

Mahale’s Kachi Ghani Groundnut Oil is extracted by pressing the seeds without the use of any artificial refining agents. Kachi Ghani Groundnut Oil is manufactured by the old traditional method by using wooden ghani (Marachekku in Tamil). These Oils contain all the original nutrition and flavor and has fresh aroma. In Wooden Ghani the oil is not heated during the manufacturing process, so there is no loss in nutrition. These are the oils which gave Strength, Stamina and long life to our Ancestors.
Available Packing: 500ml Bottle, 1ltr Bottle, 5 ltr Can

Kachi Ghani Coconut Oil

Mahale’s Pure Cold-Pressed Coconut Oil is known for its antiviral and antibacterial activity, as well as anti-inflammatory benefits.We serve the Coconut Oil Extracted from Wooden Ghani (Marachekku in Tamil) with no chemicals added.Oil is extracted from selected and best quality coconuts.This Oil contains oure and fresh aroma of coconuts.

For making Mahale’s Kachi Ghani Til Oil or Sesame Oil the seeds are put inside a Traditional Ghani i.e Wodeen Ghani to extract their Oils. This process achieves an Oil that is as close to the true essence of Sesame as possible. The Oil retains the fresh aroma and taste of Sesame Seeds.
